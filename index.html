Welcome to NotaZodiac

Akan names are derived from Ghanaian culture where children are given their first name as a 'day name' corresponding to the day in the week they were born. This web application calculates the day of the week you were born and depending on your gender outputs a name.

Enter your date of birth and select a gender to find out what your name would be.
